Who are we?

Members of the Makers Club carry many traits, although we are most noticed for; entrepreneurship, creativity, leadership and passion.

What do we do?

At the Makers Club we focus on helping anyone with creative projects and entrepreneurial ideas. Whether that means building a 3D replica of a spaceship with wood, or starting your own business is up to you.

Where do we meet up?

The Makers Club currently resides in the Georgian College ChangeMaker Space in room E212. You can check out a map of the college with our location marked, here on our about page!

Want to learn more?

Go view our about page for information about Makers Club members and execs, the Makers Space, and how to find us!

ABOUT

Equipment

In the Makers Space there is all kinds of cutting edge technology available for visitors use such as a couple 3D Printers and a Laser Cutter / Engraver. We also have monitors available for use aswell as Raspberry Pi's, Sphero's and many other gadgets that you are able to take out and tinker with!

Projects

We like to call the Makers Space an "incubater" of amazing products from amazing people. To name a couple; a fully mechanical arm has been built with a full chest piece eventually being complete as an exo-skeleton aswell as some fancy toilet paper dispenser.

Resources

The Makers Space has many resources to offer to anyone who comes in. Other students host peer seminars, on anything from version control with Git, to responsive web development. We also have peer tutoring available to anyone who needs help with their projects or tests.
